AI Agents will be going out into the world, acting on behalf of their employers, interacting with other AI Agents, web services and with people. But how will we ever trust the bots?

With honest.bot you will know:

  • Who is the AI Agent working for?
  • What was that AI told to do?
  • What permissions had been granted to the AI agent to do its task?
  • What AI technology (and technology provider) is running the Agent?
Julia Social’s innovative new identity technology allows these questions to be answered in a secure, cryptographically verifiable way. And delegation capabilities allow for multi-agent systems where authorization verifiably flows from human to an orchestrator to specialized agents to the MCP tools, A2A external agents and even humans.
Watch this space for more information as we get closer to the release of honest.bot!
