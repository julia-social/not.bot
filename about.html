About

AI Changes Everything

How will you know what’s real now that the internet is full of deepfakes and bots?

The world has changed. Over any kind of digital connection, bots can be indistinguishable from humans. How will we maintain human-to-human, social relationships in a digital world full of bots? And how can we distinguish humans from bots without gathering a lot of very personal information about them? Julia Social is on a mission to privacy-protecting digital identities that people can use to interact with other humans online.

We named the company Julia after the one character in George Orwell’s book 1984 that was able to maintain her personal agency in a world dominated by “Big Brother” surveillance by protecting her privacy.

At Julia Social, we think a lot about what would happen if we got hacked and every bit of data was stolen and made public. What could the attackers learn and use? Protecting people’s privacy starts with protecting their privacy from ourselves at Julia Social.
